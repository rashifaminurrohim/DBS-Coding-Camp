Head over to check the wiki rules out and then sign up/log in to start editing. There are currently 7,706 edits, 135 articles, 3,847 files and 6 users since its creation in May 2022.


NewJeans (Hangul: 뉴진스) is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR, consisting of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin and Hyein. They made their debut on August 1, 2022, with their first EP, New Jeans. On November 29, NewJeans terminated their exclusive contracts with ADOR after disputing with the company over HYBE's internal sabotage of the group.
